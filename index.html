// About

How do people
speak to AI?

"Don't work on the surface. Say 'Why'."

I started with a question: when someone picks up their phone and types a prompt into an AI, what are they really doing? What emotions, relationships, and social cues are embedded in those words?

My research lives at the intersection of AI engineering and Human-Computer Interaction. I study how emotional and relational context shapes the way people formulate AI queries—and what that means for next-generation agent design.

Currently pursuing an MS in Management of Technology at Hanyang University's Graduate School of Technology (MOT), where I published at ACM CHI SRC 2026.

Published Work

ACM CHI SRC 2026 · Accepted

Meaning over Quantity: How Relationship Intimacy Shapes Giver Query Formulation in LLM-based Gift Recommendation

Drawing on Construal Level Theory, we demonstrated that givers in close relationships produce contextually rich, emotionally loaded prompts, while distant-relationship givers construct sparse, norm-centered queries—with design implications for Relational Adaptive UX.

View Paper →

HCI Korea 2026 · Oral Presentation

Explaining Intent: How LLM Explanation Style Shapes Giver Perception in Gift Recommendation

Compared three LLM explanation modes (intent-based, profile-based, feature-based) across givers' product-situation fit perception and preference. Intent-centered explanations outperformed on relational congruence; feature-based excelled on comprehension depth.

Korean Ergonomics Society 2025 · Student Paper Award

Cognitive Load vs. Comprehension: GPT-based Mobile Gift Recommendation Explanations

Exploratory study examining how gift-intention-based AI explanations minimized information overload while feature-focused explanations maximized product comprehension—revealing an efficiency–effectiveness trade-off for adaptive interface design.

🏅 Encouragement Award

Beyond DX: Toward AI-nization — An Evidence-based Analysis of Generative AI Adoption Effects

A case-based integrated analysis examining the conditions under which organizations transition from digital transformation to genuine AI-nization, with strategic implications for enterprise AI deployment.
